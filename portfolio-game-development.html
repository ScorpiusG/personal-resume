Game Jam Works

  • Global Game Jam 2020

    • This year's theme was repair. My team and I of five have made Fantastic Amish in the Sky with Unity 2019. It is a multi-tasking game where the player must control two characters to fly and maintain the aircraft. I teamed with one other programmer to work on its gameplay and animations.

  • Global Game Jam 2018

    • This year's theme was transmission. My team and I of three have made Crazy Car Challenge with Unity 2017. It is an intense two-player versus game in which each player has his own gearbox to manipulate his car with. The levels and the gearbox functions are random each match, so it becomes a challenge to adapt to the gearbox controls immediately before the other player does. I am the one and only programmer in the group to work out all of its functions.

  • Ludum Dare 39

    • The theme was Running out of Power. My team and I have made Zap Energy with Unity. The player has to fly around in space like in a shmup and collect batteries to fly as far as possible while avoiding obstacles.

  • Global Game Jam 2017

    • This year's theme was waves. My team and I of six have made The Abyss with Unity 5. You would play as a submarine in which you must navigate through a dark maze to find four artifacts. The submarine can use its sonar to map out its surroundings. However, there are other creatures lurking in the dark to wreck your submarine when they are uncovered with the sonar. I worked with two other programmer to make its gameplay functions work as intended by the designer.

  • Asylum Jam 2015

    • Pumpkin Shooter, a vertical bullet-hell shooter made within 48 hours. This was made by me and a friend with Unity 5. The theme of the jam was to create a horror-based game following the rule not to use asylums nor mental institutes/hospitals to scare others.
